DIEREVA

A musical project by Gigi Giustiniani

Mycelial Electronic Downtempo · Milan, Italy

Electronic musician and documentary filmmaker. Diereva weaves analogue synthesis, field recordings and live improvisation into cinematic, introspective soundscapes.

Artist Statement

Artistic Practice

Diereva is rooted in the idea of music as a living ecosystem: layered, interconnected, and in constant flux. The project draws on organic field recordings, analogue synthesis, and the unpredictable energy of live improvisation.

The music moves through the territories of organic downtempo — shifting between ambient drifts and tighter rhythms — while maintaining a consistently tactile and cinematic character.
